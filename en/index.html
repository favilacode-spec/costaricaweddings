About

Real stories, natural light

I'm Fabian, a photographer specializing in social events, mainly weddings. My work is built on capturing authentic moments with an emotional eye — images that tell a story, reflect each couple's personality and convey what that day truly feels like. I combine technique, creativity and sensitivity to create photographs that last. Together with my team, we aim to capture the best day of your life as naturally and spontaneously as possible. Available for weddings throughout all of Costa Rica.
