About me

Pura Vida

I'm Fabian, a photographer specializing in social events, mainly weddings. My work is built on capturing authentic moments with an emotional eye — images that tell a story, reflect each couple's personality and convey what that day truly feels like. I combine technique, creativity and sensitivity to create photographs that last. Together with my team, we aim to capture the best day of your life as naturally and spontaneously as possible. Available for weddings and sessions around the world.

Process

How we work

Every image is meant to tell the real story of your wedding, with all the emotion, naturalness and sensitivity it deserves. From getting ready to the last hug, I follow every moment with a discreet, attentive eye.

1

Personalized planning

Before the big day we meet to hear your story, understand what matters most to you, and map out every detail of the coverage. Nothing is left to chance.

2

Natural, unobtrusive coverage

During the wedding I capture spontaneous moments without interrupting what's happening. I move discreetly, following the pace of the event.

3

Emotion in every photograph

I don't look for forced poses. I look for the looks, gestures and genuine emotions that tell your story exactly as it happened.

4

Careful, professional delivery

You'll get a preview within the first 3 to 5 business days after the event, and the full gallery within 30 business days. Albums are also available if you'd like a keepsake.

FAQ

Frequently asked questions

Do we meet before the wedding?

Yes — we set up a call or meet in person to get to know each other, plan the coverage, and make sure I capture what matters most to you.

Do you shoot candid or posed photos?

Both. During the event I focus on natural, documentary-style shots, and only step in when needed — for group photos or a specific portrait.

What happens if it rains or something runs late?

I adapt to the real pace of the event. Coverage is flexible and I'm ready for changes in weather or timing.

Do you work alone or with an assistant?

It depends on the event — for larger weddings I bring an assistant and however many photographers are needed.

Can we see full past galleries?

Of course! I'm happy to share private galleries from real weddings so you can see my style from start to finish.
